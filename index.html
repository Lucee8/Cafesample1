About The Fika Cafe

Kalwa's Favourite Spot for Flavor & Vibes

Tucked away on 60 Feet Road in Kalwa West, The Fika Cafe was born from a simple idea — that great food, a warm atmosphere, and good company are all you need for a perfect time. We've become the go-to hangout for couples, families, and friend groups across Thane.

From cheesy pizzas and juicy burgers to loaded sandwiches and indulgent Italian dishes — every plate is made with love and served with the kind of warm, quick service that keeps our regulars coming back every week.
